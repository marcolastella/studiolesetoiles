ABOUT
SARA & MARCO

In 2013 a new photographic project comes to life:
“Les Etoiles Studio”
thanks to the merging of two artist,
Sara Busi and Marco Lastella, in Milan.

During these years, the project boasts to have worked with important exponents of fashion, singers, actors and many more; specializing, therefore, in fashion photography, glamour and portraiture.

Constantly looking for inspiration,
we let art be the guide to our passion.
